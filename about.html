About Us

ZILO TECH

From the heart of Bamenda, Cameroon, a spark of innovation is igniting the world. Zilo, a tech startup with a team as passionate as they are brilliant, is weaving technology with a touch of humanity. Their first creation, Z-learn, is more than just an app - it's a bridge to a brighter future, crafted with the belief that everyone deserves access to quality education. Zilo's story is one of hope, empowerment, and the boundless potential that lies within Cameroon's tech scene. Stay tuned, because with Zilo, the future is looking bright.
